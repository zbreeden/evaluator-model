Market Basket & ROI Metrics

Capture: portion of potential lift realized through shelf or promotion changes.

Support: frequency of A and B appearing together in transactions.

Confidence: probability of B given A.

Lift: ratio of confidence to B’s base rate; >1 means positive association.

Market Basket Proportions: base share of each item in all baskets.
